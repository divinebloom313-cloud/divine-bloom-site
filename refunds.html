Refund Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. Our Guarantee

We want you to love your tea. If you're not satisfied with your order, reach out within 14 days of delivery and we'll do our best to make it right.

2. Unopened Products

Unopened, unused tea in its original packaging may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a full refund. Please contact us at divinebloom313@gmail.com to start a return.

3. Opened Products

Because our teas are consumable goods, opened packages are not eligible for return. If something about an opened product genuinely wasn't right — quality issue, wrong item received, damaged in transit — contact us and we'll work with you directly.

4. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, or you received the wrong item, please email divinebloom313@gmail.com within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the item. We'll send a replacement or issue a refund.

5. Refund Timing

Once a return is received and approved, refunds are issued to your original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear, depending on your bank or card provider.

6. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (damaged, defective, or incorrect item), in which case we'll cover it.

7. Contact

To start a return or ask about a refund, email divinebloom313@gmail.com.
