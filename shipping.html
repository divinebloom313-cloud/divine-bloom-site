Shipping Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. Processing Time

Orders are typically prepared and packed within 1–3 business days of purchase. Each tea is measured and packaged in small batches, so processing may take slightly longer during high-volume periods or holidays — we'll always let you know if there's a delay.

2. Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. Standard domestic delivery typically takes 3–7 business days after processing. Exact rates and carrier options will be presented before you complete your purchase.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information so you can follow your package's journey to your door.

4. Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship within the United States. If you're located outside the U.S. and would like to place an order, please reach out to us directly at divinebloom313@gmail.com to see what's possible.

5. Lost or Damaged Packages

If your order arrives damaged or appears to be lost in transit, please contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date at divinebloom313@gmail.com so we can help make it right.

6. Address Accuracy

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We're not able to reroute packages once they've shipped, so we recommend confirming your address is complete and correct before submitting your order.

7. Contact

Questions about a shipment can be sent to divinebloom313@gmail.com.
